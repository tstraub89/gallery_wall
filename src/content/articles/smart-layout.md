# Understanding Smart Layout: AI-Powered Gallery Wall Design

Staring at a pile of frames wondering how they'll fit together? GalleryPlanner's **Smart Layout** feature uses AI algorithms to generate professional-quality gallery wall arrangements in seconds. This guide explains how it works, when to use it, and how to get the best results.

## What is Smart Layout?

Smart Layout is an AI-powered tool that automatically arranges your frame inventory into aesthetically pleasing, balanced layouts. Instead of manually dragging and dropping frames for hours, Smart Layout does the heavy lifting‚Äîgenerating dozens of layout options based on proven design principles.

**Think of it as:** Having a professional interior designer instantly create custom layouts tailored to your exact frame collection.

## How Smart Layout Works

Under the hood, Smart Layout uses **advanced computational algorithms** to solve the gallery wall layout problem. Here's what happens when you click "Generate":

### 1. **Input Analysis**

The algorithm reads your frame inventory:
- Frame dimensions (width √ó height)
- Quantity of each frame size
- Wall dimensions and constraints

### 2. **Algorithm Selection**

You can choose from 5 specialized algorithms (or let "Auto" pick the best one):

- **Grid Generator:** Organizes frames into perfect rows and columns
- **Masonry Generator:** Creates a brick-like, staggered pattern
- **Skyline Generator:** Aligns frames along top or bottom edges
- **Monte Carlo Generator:** Uses randomized placement with visual optimization
- **Spiral Generator:** Arranges frames radiating outward from a center point

### 3. **Layout Generation**

The algorithm places frames on your wall while respecting:

- **Spacing rules:** 2-3 inches between all frames (industry standard)
- **Visual balance:** Distributing visual weight evenly across the composition
- **Collision detection:** Ensuring frames don't overlap
- **Wall boundaries:** Keeping all frames within your wall dimensions

### 4. **Optimization**

The algorithm evaluates the layout based on:

- **Symmetry vs. asymmetry** (depending on the algorithm)
- **Negative space distribution** (avoiding crowding)
- **Focal point prominence** (highlighting the largest or most important frame)

### 5. **Result**

You get a production-ready layout in seconds. Don't love it? Click "Generate" again for a completely new arrangement‚Äîeach run produces different results (except Grid, which is deterministic).

- **Mix orientations:** Have both portrait and landscape options

You don't have to accept the layout exactly as generated:

1. Generate a Smart Layout
2. **Manually adjust** frames that feel slightly off
3. **Swap frames** for different sizes if needed
4. **Fine-tune spacing** for your personal preference

### Tip 5: Adjust Wall Size if Layouts Feel Cramped

If Smart Layout keeps producing crowded arrangements:

- Your wall might be too small for your frame inventory
- Try reducing the number of frames in your library
- Or increase your wall dimensions to give frames more breathing room

**Rule of thumb:** Your frames should cover about 60-70% of your wall area. If Smart Layout uses 90%+, you likely have too many frames for that wall size.

## Behind the Scenes: The Technical Details

For the data nerds and algorithm enthusiasts:

### Monte Carlo Generator

Based on the **Monte Carlo method**, this algorithm uses random sampling and statistical optimization:

1. Randomly places frames on the wall
2. Evaluates the layout quality using a scoring function (considers balance, spacing, overlap)
3. Runs thousands of iterations, keeping the best-scoring layout
4. Uses simulated annealing to escape local optima

**Why it's powerful:** Can find creative solutions that deterministic algorithms miss.

---

### Skyline Generator

Inspired by the **skyline packing problem** from computer science:

1. Maintains a "skyline" (horizontal edge) as frames are placed
2. Places frames left-to-right, always aligning to the skyline
3. Updates the skyline as each frame is added
4. Optimizes for minimal height variance

**Why it's powerful:** Guarantees clean horizontal alignment while maximizing space efficiency.

---

### Grid Generator

Uses a **bin-packing algorithm** to fit frames into grid cells:

1. Calculates optimal grid cell size based on frame dimensions
2. Assigns frames to cells, minimizing empty space
3. Centers the entire grid on the wall
4. Ensures uniform spacing between all frames

**Why it's powerful:** Produces clean, predictable results perfect for symmetry-lovers.

## Troubleshooting Smart Layout

### "Smart Layout says 'Not enough frames.'"

**Solution:** Add at least 4 frames to your library. The algorithms need sufficient inventory to generate meaningful layouts.

---

### "All my frames overlap."

**Solution:** Your frames are too large for your wall. Either:
- Reduce the number of frames in your library
- Increase your wall dimensions
- Remove a few of the largest frames

---

### "The layout looks too crowded."

**Solution:** 
- Manually remove 1-2 frames after generation
- Try a different algorithm (Skyline or Grid tend to be less dense than Masonry or Monte Carlo)
- Increase wall size

---

### "I keep getting the exact same layout."

**Solution:** You're likely using the **Grid** algorithm, which is deterministic (always produces the same result for the same inputs). Try **Monte Carlo** or **Masonry** for variation.
